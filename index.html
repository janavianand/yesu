About YES - U

Youth Empowerment Summit Unlimited, Inc, (Y.E.S. - U) is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that has been an integral part of Essex, and Union Counties . Y.E.S. was founded by Dr. Ellis, Ph. D., an accomplished educator who retired after serving in the public and privates school systems to open the Y.E.S.- U Outreach Center. He continues to serve full- time as its executive director. Since its inception in 1997, Y.E.S.- U has served over 4,000 youths through mentoring programs, college prep activities, workshops and various community events, with the help of over 300 faithful volunteers. Evaluations reveal that 85% of our participants successfully complete our programs. Over 98 % of girls do not become pregnant while in our programs, and self-reporting evaluations reveal that they understand, embrace and practice the abstinence lifestyle upon program completion.

This summer I interned in the YES U experience at McMillan Entertainment. It was an amazing and eye opening experience, and I learned something new every day that I was here. I got the opportunity to co-host my own radio show, which is something that I have wanted to learn how to do for a very long time. The most rewarding part of this experience was getting to be creative with the content, and then hear the results of the hard work that was put in to make it happen. Being in a professional studio setting allowed me to see what the industry is like from a real world perspective. I also got the opportunity to engineer shows, get involved in a variety of different shows that run at different paces, and create other types of content, such as promos, commercials, and even a video. This was an experience that more than lived up to my expectations, one that I highly recommend trying, and one that I would do again in a heart beat.
